The Upcoming Release Of "Baahubali: Crown Of Blood"



After the massive success of the "Baahubali" movie series, plans were underway to expand the franchise into a series. Initially, a live-action web series titled "Baahubali: Before the Beginning" was in development, based on the book "The Rise Of Sivagami" by writer Anand Neelakanta. However, Netflix halted the project after shooting some episodes, leaving fans eagerly awaiting further developments.

In a surprising turn of events, the creators of "Baahubali" have now announced a shift towards an animated web series titled "Baahubali: Crown Of Blood." SS Rajamouli, the mastermind behind the "Baahubali" series, has personally announced the upcoming release of the animated series.
